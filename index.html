🌏 Language & Culture

Immersive Chinese Learning Environment

Our programme is conducted fully in Mandarin, providing a natural and engaging Chinese-speaking environment.

We are not a language tuition centre. Instead, through character education and practical life experiences, children use, understand, and express Mandarin in meaningful real-life contexts.

For children growing up in an English-dominant education system, this provides a supportive and steady platform to strengthen Chinese communication skills.

🌟 Our Learning Approach

Not instruction — but experience.

We adopt an experiential learning approach rather than one-way teaching. Children learn through participation and grow through practice — building confidence and developing responsibility.

  • Story-based learning and guided reflection
  • Interactive activities and team challenges
  • Practical life applications
  • Home extension tasks and parent engagement

A place where children grow in character, and families grow together.

Our Beginning

Dexuan Children’s Development Programme was founded with the intention of providing children with a stable, warm, and purposeful environment for growth.

In a rapidly changing society, children face challenges in self-discipline, emotional regulation, interpersonal relationships, and value formation. Through structured curriculum design, we aim to support children in building a strong foundation of character and life skills.

What We Believe

During a child’s formative years, character matters more than grades. Values matter more than achievements.

Our programme centres on moral development and character formation, using practical life applications and experiential learning to guide children in developing sound judgment and responsibility.

We believe that stable values and strong character form the foundation for navigating the future with confidence.

Our Core Philosophy

Built upon three foundations:

  • Character Development
  • Life Skills & Emotional Growth
  • Immersive Chinese Language Environment

Our Vision

We aspire to create a safe, structured, and purposeful learning environment where children gain confidence and families find shared values.

Dexuan Children’s Development Programme aims to be a community rooted in family values — a place where meaningful transformation unfolds gradually and deeply.

Our Team

The programme is organised and facilitated by a team of professionals and volunteers who are passionate about children’s development and character education.

With years of experience in family education and value-based development, our team collaborates in curriculum planning, facilitation, and programme review to ensure continuous improvement.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • A safe and orderly learning environment
  • Clear programme structure and direction
  • Respect for each child’s individuality
  • Transparent communication with parents
  • Ongoing reflection and enhancement
